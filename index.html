"I design best when I actually talk to the people who'll use the thing."

Constança eco

I design products people actually use — and I can explain why each decision matters.

I am finishing a Master's in Computer Science at Instituto Superior Técnico, focused on interaction design and UX research. I love turning messy user problems into clear product decisions.

My thesis work combines heuristic evaluation, task performance, and perceived usability. In practice, that means I do not stop at "this looks better"; I focus on whether the change helps users complete real tasks with less effort.

I can also ship frontend when needed, but my main value is product thinking: understanding constraints, prioritizing the right problems, and connecting design choices to measurable outcomes. Based in Lisbon, open to remote and relocation.

Research & Method

My process is simple: define the user problem, design the smallest decision that solves it, and track at least one outcome metric (measured or clearly estimated). That is how I frame every project in this portfolio.
