The Recipe Codex

Craft

Ingredients

UX Research generous handful
Heuristic Evaluation 1 cup, leveled
User Interviews to taste
Usability Testing 2 tablespoons
Affinity Mapping a pinch

Method

  1. 01Listen until you understand the real problem — not the one you were told to solve
  2. 02Map the space until patterns stop being coincidences
  3. 03Test early, test often — with actual humans, not assumptions
  4. 04Measure at least one outcome — always

"I don't stop at 'this looks better' — I ask whether the change helps users complete real tasks with less effort."

Ingredients

Design Systems foundation layer
Wireframing sketch first
Interaction Design to taste
Figma daily driver

Method

  1. 01Sketch quickly — don't commit to pixels until the idea earns it
  2. 02Build components that engineers actually want to use
  3. 03Design for edge cases, not just the happy path
  4. 04Document the decisions, not just the outcomes

"Every element should earn its place. Design is not decoration."

Ingredients

HTML · CSS · JS production-ready
Tailwind CSS 1 cup
GSAP · CSS Animation as needed
CS Background foundational

Method

  1. 01Prototype in the browser when Figma isn't enough
  2. 02Talk to engineers as a peer, not a client
  3. 03Understand what 'technically feasible' actually means
  4. 04Ship it — design that lives in Figma doesn't count

"My main value is product thinking — but I can implement when needed."

Ingredients

Product Strategy 2 tablespoons
Prioritisation always first
Stakeholder Comms clear and direct

Method

  1. 01Understand the business constraint before proposing solutions
  2. 02Prioritise by user impact + technical effort
  3. 03Communicate decisions, not just designs
  4. 04Iterate without ego — the best idea wins

"I focus on whether the change helps users complete real tasks with less effort."

Serving suggestion: best with ambitious, values-driven teams who trust the process.

Self

I design user experiences grounded in research. Currently finishing a Master's in Computer Science at Instituto Superior Técnico, where I focus on measuring what matters: task completion rates, cognitive load reduction, and whether design choices actually help people work faster.

My work spans product strategy, interaction design, and UX research. I've shipped features for live platforms, led design systems work, and tested designs with real users. Based in Lisbon, open to remote and relocation opportunities. Languages: PT · EN · FR · ES
